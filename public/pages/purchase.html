Use this Gallery on your Website

If you are an ambituous or professional photographer and like what you see, you can use the gallery component used on this website for your own project(s)!

The software, which I have built from scratch (based on pure JavaScript in both front- and backend) comes with a flickr-plugin to automatically synchronize images from any photographer platform or image cloud service with the ones on any web page. Additional plugins for the integration of other services (e.g. Adobe Cloud, 500px, Photo Bucket, Instagram etc.) can be implemented on demand.

Please get in touch with me in order to find out how to integrate this gallery plugin into your website, (wordpress) blog or mobile application.

I will be happy to answer your questions and will adapt the software to your need at a reasonable rate.
